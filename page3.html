This is your Ukay-ukay Madness!!!

You know how Barney Stinson always say “New is always better?” Let’s challenge that. It can’t be stressed enough how underrated second-hand buying really is. It’s usually scoffed at by most Filipinos, but buying used items is a lot more rewarding.
Admittedly, we have this stigma on buying used goods. It’s time to break that stigma now because you’ll be missing out on the benefits. You should try it too!!
